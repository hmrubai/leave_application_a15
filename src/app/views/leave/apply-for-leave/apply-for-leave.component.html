Leave Policy of BacBon Limited (Effective from 1st April 2022).

  1. Annual leave will be available after 01 years of service. After one year of service, an employee will get 1.11 days AL per month, considering 01 day's leave for every 18 days of work. 

  2. An employee must apply for AL at least 02-day in advance. 

  3. AL will be carried forward.

  4. Casual leave is available from the beginning of the employment. An employee is entitled to 0.83-day CL every month. CL is only applicable for any acceptable emergency case. CL is not available for the consecutive days. 

  5. Sick leave is available from the beginning of the employment. An employee is entitled to 1.17-day SL every month. When applying for SL for more than 03 days, an employee must produce medical documents. 

  6. Casual and sick leave will not be carried forward. It will be replenished at the end of every December. 

  7. If an employee is sick, injured, or unable to work, he/she must notify the supervisor & HRD preferably by SMS, by 9.0 a.m. on the Ô¨Årst day of absence. Employees should state the causes for his/her absence.

  8. Employees should apply for leave of absence to the supervisor immediately (within 24 hours) for approval, and the supervisor should forward it to HRD.

  9. Employees should check medical advice for sickness, injury, or contact with a contagious disease whenever possible. BacBon Ltd. maintains the right to ask an employee to consult a doctor during any medical absence and notify the insurance company for the medical benefits.

  10. Any false statement an employee makes regarding absences due to medication incapacity or contact with contagious diseases may lead to an investigation. BacBon Ltd. may take disciplinary action in the event of falsifying sickness information.

  11. An employee can apply for a maximum 10-day leave in Liu (in exchange for working on a holiday) in a calendar year. 

  12. An employee can request for a maximum 10-day home/remote office for a calendar year for personal reason maintaining top most security of company documents and accessories. An employee must approve a concrete plan & action with supervisor or manager for home office days and must submit an everyday report to the supervisor/manager including HRD. Applicable reasons for home office request might be a visit outside of the country, natural disaster, risk of spreading contagious disease, etc.  

  13. A female employee will get 120-day maternity leave (maximum) fulfilling the following conditions:

  • Work tenure in the company must be at least 6 months. 

  • Have not more than two children 

  1. During maternity leave, every month an employee will get 50% of gross payment deducting conveyance amount. After the end of the maternity leave and resuming the job, rest 50% of the monthly pay will be paid upon production of childbirth proof with the application. 

  2. A male employee will get maximum 7-day paternity leave including holidays

  • Work tenure in the company must be at least 6 months. 

  • Have not more than two children 

  1. An employee will get maximum 7-day wedding leave including holidays.

Maternity and paternity leave conditions are not applicable for employed persons in a managerial and administrative roles.
